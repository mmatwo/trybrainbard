Sharpen Your Mental Math Skills

Brain Bard helps you improve mental calculation abilities through practice, timed challenges, and progress tracking. Train your brain to perform calculations faster and more accurately.

Master the math that matters in minutes a day.

Practice Mode

Answer questions at your own pace with no time limits. Customize operators and digit length from 1-5 digits to match your skill level.

Sprint Mode

Challenge yourself to answer as many questions as possible within a time limit. Customize operators, factor lengths, and test duration.

Progress Log

Track your lifetime statistics organized by difficulty tiers. Monitor your improvement over time and identify areas for growth.

Estimation Mode

Develop practical approximation skills for real-world situations. Make your best guess and see how close you were to the exact answer.
